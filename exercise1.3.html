Description: Computer Networks module focuses on developing practical networking skills alongside deeper understanding of network
principles and protocols (wired and wireless). Although the necesssary background is introduced as appropriate, the course on the whole
deals with problem-solving using current and emergent network hardware and protocols along with the development of applications to
exploit these technologies.
